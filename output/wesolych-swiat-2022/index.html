Laminate Film Terms

Thermal Laminating Film: Laminating film consisting of 2 layers. Base film made of polyester (PET) and adhesive layer made of polyethylene (PE). Usually thermal film is made in BOPL (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene Laminating Film) but can sometimes be made from PET (Polyester Laminating Film). All our thermal laminating rolls are made in our factory in the UK.

Pressure-sensitive Laminates: Mostly used in applications where high bond strength is required. Overlaying film over digital prints specially when the print has a high ink coverage. The adhesive creates a strong bond between the ink and the base film (mostly vinyl/PVC).

Laminating Film Sizes: Our laminating films are produced with a width from 100 mm up to 2000 mm. Contact us for unusual sizes of laminating film.
